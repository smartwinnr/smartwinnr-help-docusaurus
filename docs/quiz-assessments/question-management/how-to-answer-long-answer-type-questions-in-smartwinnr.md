## SmartWinnr has a descriptive-type question where you will have to write down the answer to the question in one line or paragraph  

You can follow the below steps to answer such Long - answer type Questions in SmartWinnr.

* Click on the assigned quiz to start the quiz. That will open the selected quiz.

![](/img/quizzes/helpscout-image-117d8ee0.png)

* Click on the button  **START QUIZ** to start answering the questions in the quiz.

![](/img/quizzes/helpscout-image-ee44b8f4.png)

* When you have a long answer question in your quiz, you can find the question in the following format. Here, you will have a textbox where you can enter your detailed answer to the questions.

![](/img/quizzes/helpscout-image-ede7e426.png)

While writing your Long answer, you can also use the **Text Formatting features**.

You can use the Bold, Italics, and Underline text feature to focus on keywords present in the answer.

H1 and H2 are the heading font feature to convert your text into a heading and _Tx_ can convert the text into a small size.

You can also use the list option to mention the point numbers as an ordered list or you can choose the unordered list (instead of a number, it will come as a point). There is a feature to move your context to left and right size based on your requirement.

* Once you complete answering all the questions, click on the **SUBMIT QUIZ** button to submit the quiz.

![](https://lh6.googleusercontent.com/-68Tjm_sTZ30XPOdnyKSuCW2i3qAjFiBnaUsnHuW6vn7dAQnuiwaYn4xYmQk02eR0_KZhpNxMl5wqYtD-qHyws1rSdHCIYs4sunLwsYk4e5KTCS0xK2VZR0Cds2Sw09jEUwhD1oq)

* After submitting the quiz, youﾂwill be redirected to the quiz leaderboard page. In case **if all the questions in your quiz were long answer questions, the quiz leaderboard will show zero scores until the evaluator evaluates your answers.**

![](/img/quizzes/helpscout-image-8ba1ab95.png)

* Once the **evaluation is done**, the **score will be visible** on the leaderboard.

## ﾂ How to view the Quiz score and Feedback?

* Once your evaluator evaluates and gives points for each question, your scores and ranking will be available toﾂyou on the quiz leaderboard.
* To check the leaderboard, select the quiz from the **Completed section tab** and select the quiz.

![](/img/quizzes/helpscout-image-54a3632f.png)

* By selecting the quiz, you can view the score leaderboard.

![](/img/quizzes/helpscout-image-d1e4e4c0.png)

* You can also check the evaluator's feedback by clicking on the **Review** icon at the top right corner.

![](/img/quizzes/helpscout-image-ad2bcfb4.png)

* It will redirect you to the following page, where you can access the answers and feedback provided by the editor.

![](/img/quizzes/helpscout-image-3d06bfd8.png)

* **User Response:** This is the answer submitted by the user during the quiz.
* **Evaluator Feedback:** This feedback is created with the right answer for the Long Answer question before the quiz has started.
* **Additional Feedback:** This feedback is based on the user's response after the quiz is finished.
