Follow the below steps to view the SmatPath analytics:

Go to EDITOR >  **LEARNING AND KNOWLEDGE > SmartPaths > View All SmartPaths.** It will open the following page displaying all the created SmartPaths.

[![](/img/learning/helpscout-image-ebf41726.png)](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5fcc5d22d580ce55a38b4a61/images/6016a10d2042ff6d1b2a9043/file-kfMOibsmCo.png)

_Click_ on a specific SmartPath to view its analytics. It opens the following page:

[![](/img/learning/helpscout-image-79c6fe7e.png)](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5fcc5d22d580ce55a38b4a61/images/6016bcf1a4cefb30ae5c5b33/file-9LCEMz1sNf.png)

On this page, _click_ on the **'View Batches'** button at the top right corner of the screen. It will open the following screen where you will see the list of all the batches assigned to this SmartPath.

[![](/img/learning/helpscout-image-018f9c48.png)](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5fcc5d22d580ce55a38b4a61/images/6016bed06867724dfc6ede34/file-tw4DQSIhpy.png)

 Now, _click_ on the **'Analytics'** icon of a particular batch. It will open the following SmartPath analytics report:

[![](/img/learning/helpscout-image-d4d70690.png)](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5fcc5d22d580ce55a38b4a61/images/6016bfd81f25b9041beb9c09/file-8M11BPOFRc.png)

#### This report contains the following sections:

1

####    Overall Participation:

This shows how many participants have completed the SmartPath, how many are still progressing through it, and how many of them haven't started it yet. All this data is displayed in the form of a donut graph:

[![](/img/learning/helpscout-image-29f582d5.png)](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5fcc5d22d580ce55a38b4a61/images/6016c17ba4cefb30ae5c5b3e/file-Jyl9VeOnSN.png)

2

####     Participation By Country:

This displays a bar graph that shows the percentage of participants who have started the SmartPath by country.

[![](/img/learning/helpscout-image-e22359d0.png)](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5fcc5d22d580ce55a38b4a61/images/6016c8881f25b9041beb9c14/file-TA775tfN5B.png)

4

####     Quiz Analytics

##### Categorywise performance for all countries: 

This section gives a bar graph that shows the category-wise performance of all the countries in a quiz.

![](https://support.smartwinnr.com/api/v1/attachments/845)

##### Performance by Category by Country:

This section gives a heat map that shows the participant's performance in the quiz by category by country.

![](https://support.smartwinnr.com/api/v1/attachments/638)

5

####     Analytics By Modules and Segments

[![](/img/learning/helpscout-image-b5e10ed5.png)](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5fcc5d22d580ce55a38b4a61/images/6016d8a72042ff6d1b2a907c/file-tTcZ8726vy.png)

_Clicking on the analytics icon of a particular segment gives you detailed analytics of that segment._

If you click to see the analytics of a quiz it will display the following analytics report of that quiz:

[![](/img/learning/helpscout-image-8c198056.png)](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5fcc5d22d580ce55a38b4a61/images/6016db6c2042ff6d1b2a9082/file-9AQrUX6cKI.png)

Here you can see Overall Participation, Participation by Country, You can also see the leaderboard and various other metrics.

[![](/img/learning/helpscout-image-12a22a72.png)](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5fcc5d22d580ce55a38b4a61/images/6016dc9fa4cefb30ae5c5b72/file-dfwm5xdOnY.png)

You can also view metrics of SmartFeeds, Surveys, etc., by _clicking_ on the respective analytics icon.

[![](/img/learning/helpscout-image-3e712aee.png)](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5fcc5d22d580ce55a38b4a61/images/6016ddc3ac2f834ec5383a06/file-pskRYuI4uq.png)

6

####     User Wise Performance

##### This section gives analytics for user-wise performance.

You will get to see each user's progress in the SmartPath completion.

[![](/img/learning/helpscout-image-64c4c3d8.png)](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5fcc5d22d580ce55a38b4a61/images/6016e35c1f25b9041beb9c3f/file-Q1AruF41VE.png)
