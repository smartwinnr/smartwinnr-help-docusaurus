SmartFeeds enable you to send short snippets of information to your teams. It helps to **regularly reinforce important information**, without going through lengthy training sessions. 

## What to send as SmartFeeds?

Most important information that your team needs to remember in order to function well. Like:

*   keep your team updated with the latest changes to your products, 
*   announce a new product launch,
*   about new services or offers that have been added, 
*   any piece of information that is important for them to remember. 

These SmartFeeds stay in their feeds for future reference.

## What types of files can be sent as SmartFeeds?

You can send 

*   videos (like product demonstration videos, training videos), 
*   short PDFs (like product specification sheet, clinical trial data), 
*   powerpoint presentations ( on product knowledge, FAB)
*   link to other websites, 
*   or an image or a poster

SmartFeeds come in the form of Twitter-like feeds to the teams’ SmartWinnr mobile app and web view. 

## Is the recipient notified when a SmartFeed is sent?

Participants are notified through an email and a push notification on their mobile device that a SmartFeed has been sent to them.
