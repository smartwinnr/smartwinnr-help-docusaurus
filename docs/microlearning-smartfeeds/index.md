# MicroLearning & SmartFeeds

Deliver impactful learning through bite-sized, engaging content that fits into your learners' daily workflow.

## What are SmartFeeds?

SmartFeeds are micro-learning content pieces designed for just-in-time learning. They deliver focused, actionable information in digestible formats that learners can consume quickly and apply immediately.

## Key Features

### Content Types

- **Text-based content** with rich formatting
- **Video content** with interactive elements
- **Audio content** for on-the-go learning
- **PowerPoint presentations** converted to engaging formats
- **Interactive media** with polls and quizzes

### Engagement Features

- **Social interactions** (likes, comments, sharing)
- **Progress tracking** and completion status
- **Mobile-optimized** for any device
- **Offline access** for uninterrupted learning

## Best Practices

- **Keep it Focused**: One key concept per SmartFeed
- **Make it Visual**: Use images and media to enhance understanding
- **Encourage Interaction**: Include questions or calls-to-action
- **Optimize for Mobile**: Ensure content works on all devices
- **Regular Updates**: Keep content fresh and relevant
