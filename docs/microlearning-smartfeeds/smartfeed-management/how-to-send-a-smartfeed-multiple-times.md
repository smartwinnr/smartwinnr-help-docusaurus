## Send a SmartFeed multiple times

1. Go to Smartfeeds  View All SmartFeeds.
2. From here,Â choose the SmartFeed that you want to send again.
3. On the 'View SmartFeed' page, click on the '**Send**' button at the top right.
4. Then choose the participants you want to send the SmartFeed to and click on the '**Send Smartfeed**' button. [![](/img/learning/helpscout-image-971d936b.png)](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5fcc5d22d580ce55a38b4a61/images/5ff09a946451e825e3b8f982/file-7ux8oX3IuD.png)

_You can view it from the Sent History which gets displayed on the 'View SmartFeed' page._
