Only the users with the editor profile can create a group and use it.

The Group feature allows you to assign a quiz, a smart feed or a survey to multiple users at the same time. This feature comes in handy when you have to send out content repeatedly to the same group of people in your zone or territory. You don't have to select each individual participant separately, every time you have to send the content. For example, during a training session, you would have to send multiple quizzes to the same group of users to test the knowledge that they have gained during the training.
