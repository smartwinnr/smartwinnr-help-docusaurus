After creating a Quiz if you want to open the inactive Quiz or if you want to close the quiz, follow the bellow steps:  
  
Go to Editor > LEARNING AND KNOWLEDGE > Competition > Open the desired Competition > Click on the 4th tab that says "View Tasks"

1\. In the Tasks page of the competition, you can see the quizzes in the competition.

2\. Identify the correct quiz

3\. To start a quiz, click on the Open Quiz button of a particular quiz.

4\. Click on the same Close Quiz button to close the quiz once the time is over. That will close the quiz. A user who had already started with the quiz, will be able to finish it. But a user who hasn’t started yet, will not be able to start anymore.
