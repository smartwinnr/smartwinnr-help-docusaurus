# Quiz Analytics & Reports

SmartWinnr provides detailed real-time quiz analytics to help you gain clear insights into participant performance and learning effectiveness.

## Accessing Quiz Analytics

To view detailed analytics for a specific quiz:

1. Go to **Left Menu > Editor > Questions and Quizzes > View all Quizzes**
2. Click on the **analytics icon** next to the quiz you want to analyze

## Types of Analytics & Reports

### 1. Individual Quiz Analytics

#### Overview Metrics

Each quiz analytics dashboard provides:

- **Total Participants**: Number of users who took the quiz
- **Completion Rate**: Percentage of assigned users who completed
- **Average Score**: Mean score across all participants
- **Time Spent**: Average time taken to complete
- **Pass/Fail Rate**: Based on configured passing criteria

#### Question-Level Analysis

- **Question Performance**: Which questions were answered correctly most/least often
- **Difficulty Assessment**: Identify questions that may be too easy or difficult
- **Response Distribution**: How answers were distributed across options
- **Time per Question**: Which questions took longest to answer

### 2. Team Analytics

Access team-wide performance data through **EDITOR > Questions and Quizzes > Reports > Team Analytics**.

#### Overall Performance Metrics

- **Division Performance**: Results for your entire division based on access level
- **Performance by Country**: Geographic breakdown of results
- **Leaderboard**: Top performers across all quizzes

#### Category-wise Performance

Heat map visualization showing:

- Performance by knowledge category
- Geographic distribution of category strengths/weaknesses  
- Comparative analysis across different subjects

### 3. Individual Learner Reports

#### Learner Overall Report

Comprehensive view of individual participant performance including:

- **Quiz History**: All completed quizzes and scores
- **Category Strengths**: Areas where the learner performs well
- **Improvement Areas**: Categories needing attention
- **Progress Tracking**: Performance trends over time
- **Badges Earned**: Achievements and certifications

#### Individual Quiz Performance

Detailed breakdown for specific quiz attempts:

- **Question-by-question results**
- **Time spent per question**
- **Correct/incorrect answer analysis**
- **Explanation views**: Whether learner reviewed explanations

### 4. Question Response Analytics

#### Response Pattern Analysis

- **Popular Wrong Answers**: Common misconceptions
- **Answer Distribution**: How choices were selected
- **Confidence Levels**: If enabled, participant confidence in answers
- **Review Behavior**: Which explanations were most accessed

#### Question Difficulty Metrics

- **Success Rate**: Percentage answering correctly
- **Discrimination Index**: How well question differentiates performance levels
- **Time Analysis**: Average time spent on each question
- **Skip Rate**: How often question was left unanswered

## Analytics Views & Filtering

### Setting Analytics Views

By default, analytics are grouped by **SubDivisions** (typically countries or states, depending on system configuration).

You can customize views by:

- **Geographic Region**: Country, state, or city level
- **Department**: Organizational units
- **Role**: Job functions or levels
- **Time Period**: Specific date ranges

### Advanced Filtering Options

Use the **Filter** button to slice and dice information by:

- **Country**: Geographic location
- **Category**: Knowledge areas or subjects  
- **User Tags**: Custom participant attributes
- **Email**: Specific individuals or domains
- **Date Range**: Custom time periods
- **Score Range**: Performance brackets

## Specialized Reports

### 1. Quiz Comparison Reports

Compare performance across multiple quizzes:

- **Score Distributions**: How results vary between different quizzes
- **Completion Rates**: Which quizzes have better participation
- **Time Analysis**: Relative difficulty based on completion time
- **Category Performance**: Consistent strengths/weaknesses across quizzes

### 2. Progress Reports

Track learning progression over time:

- **Individual Progress**: Personal learning journey
- **Team Progress**: Group advancement
- **Category Improvement**: Subject-specific development
- **Skill Building**: Competency development tracking

### 3. Engagement Reports

Measure participation and engagement:

- **Login Frequency**: How often users access the platform
- **Quiz Attempt Patterns**: When and how frequently users take quizzes
- **Content Interaction**: Which explanations and resources are most used
- **Mobile vs. Web Usage**: Platform preferences

- **Drill-down Capability**: Click on charts to see detailed data

- **Real-time Updates**: Live data refresh
- **Custom Date Ranges**: Flexible time period selection

## Using Analytics for Improvement

### Identifying Learning Gaps

- **Low-performing Categories**: Areas needing additional training
- **Consistent Problem Questions**: Content that may need revision
- **Regional Differences**: Location-specific training needs
- **Individual Coaching Opportunities**: Participants needing extra support

### Content Optimization

- **Question Quality Assessment**: Identify questions to improve or remove
- **Difficulty Calibration**: Adjust question difficulty based on performance
- **Content Gaps**: Areas where additional questions are needed
- **Explanation Effectiveness**: Which explanations help learning most

### Training Program Enhancement

- **Curriculum Adjustments**: Modify training based on quiz results
- **Pacing Optimization**: Adjust training speed and repetition
- **Personalized Learning Paths**: Create custom journeys based on performance
- **Intervention Strategies**: Targeted support for struggling learners

## Best Practices for Using Analytics

### Regular Review Cycle

- **Weekly Check-ins**: Monitor recent quiz performance
- **Monthly Deep Dives**: Comprehensive analysis of trends
- **Quarterly Reviews**: Strategic assessment of learning effectiveness
- **Annual Evaluation**: Overall program assessment and planning

### Action-Oriented Analysis

- **Set Performance Benchmarks**: Establish clear success criteria
- **Identify Intervention Triggers**: When to provide additional support
- **Track Improvement Actions**: Monitor effectiveness of changes
- **Celebrate Successes**: Recognize achievements and progress

### Data-Driven Decision Making

- **Evidence-Based Training**: Use analytics to guide content creation
- **Performance-Based Recognition**: Reward based on demonstrated learning
- **Targeted Interventions**: Focus resources where most needed
- **Continuous Improvement**: Regular refinement based on data insights
