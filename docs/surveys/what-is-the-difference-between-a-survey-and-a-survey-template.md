In SmartWinnr, a survey is a questionnaire that can be taken only once by each individual survey takers. You can use survey in scenarios like:

*   Conducting a pre-training survey for your trainees before they start their training. This helps you to know the background of your trainees and their expectations from your training program.
*   Conducting a post-training survey for your trainees after they finish their training. This helps you to know your trainees' thoughts, opinions, and feedback on your training.

On the other hand, a survey template is a template with a set of questionnaires that can be reused by a user. This kind of template is typically useful for the field staff when they have to survey the customers.
