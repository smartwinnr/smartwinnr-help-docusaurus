# SmartWinnr Platform Overview

Welcome to SmartWinnr, a comprehensive revenue enablement platform that helps organizations drive performance through engaging learning experiences, assessments, and coaching.

## What is SmartWinnr?

SmartWinnr is a modular platform designed to:

- **Enhance Learning**: Deliver bite-sized, engaging content through various formats
- **Assess Knowledge**: Create comprehensive quizzes and assessments to measure understanding
- **Enable Coaching**: Facilitate both video and field coaching for skill development
- **Drive Engagement**: Gamify learning through competitions and leaderboards
- **Collect Feedback**: Gather insights through surveys and forms
- **Track Performance**: Monitor progress and performance across all activities

## Available Modules

SmartWinnr offers the following modules, each designed for specific use cases:

### Core Learning Modules

- **Quiz Module**: Create and manage assessments and knowledge checks
- **SmartPath Module**: Design structured learning journeys with multiple content types
- **SmartFeed Module**: Deliver microlearning content in engaging, bite-sized formats

### Performance & Development Modules  

- **Video Coaching Module**: Enable remote coaching through video submissions and reviews
- **Field Coaching Module**: Facilitate in-person coaching and competency assessments
- **Survey Module**: Collect feedback and insights through customizable surveys

### Content & Collaboration Modules

- **Knowledge Hub Module**: Centralize knowledge sharing and resource management
- **Forms Module**: Create custom data collection forms and scorecards

### System Features

- **Cross-Module Features**: Competitions, gamification, advanced analytics
- **System Management**: User administration, permissions, and platform configuration

## Module Availability

Different organizations may have access to different modules based on their licensing agreement. This documentation is organized by module to help you quickly find information relevant to your specific platform configuration.
