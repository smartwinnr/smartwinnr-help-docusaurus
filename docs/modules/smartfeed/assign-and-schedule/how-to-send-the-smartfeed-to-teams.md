SmartWinnr allows you to send SmartFeeds to **groups or teams**, making it easy to reach a large number of users in a single step.

When you assign SmartFeeds to a group, any **new members added to the group** will automatically receive the active SmartFeeds assigned to that group. There is no need to resend these SmartFeeds to new users.

* Select the SmartFeed you want to share with your teams.
* Click the **Send** button located at the top-right corner of the SmartFeed.

![When you assign SmartFeeds to a group, any new members added to the group will automatically receive the active SmartFeeds assigned to that ](/img/helpscout/editors/how-to-edit-a-smartfeed-1.png)

This will take to the page where it asks whom do you want to send the SmartFeed.

When sending a SmartFeed, you can choose recipients using different **selection types**:

* **User**: Select individual users to receive the SmartFeed.
* **Group**: Select users based on pre-defined groups within your organization.
* **Metatag**: Select users based on metatags assigned to them.

![](/img/helpscout/editors/how-to-send-the-smartfeed-to-teams-1.png)

In addition to selecting users, groups, and metatags, you can further **filter recipients** when sending a SmartFeed:

* **Business Unit and Subdivision**: Select users belonging to a specific business unit or subdivision.
* **Role**: Filter users based on their role within the organization.
* **Email IDs**: You can paste one or more specific email IDs in the email column to target only those users.

These advanced filters allow you to **precisely control** who receives the SmartFeed, ensuring the right information reaches the right audience.

![These advanced filters allow you to precisely control who receives the SmartFeed, ensuring the right information reaches the right audience.](/img/helpscout/editors/how-to-send-the-smartfeed-to-teams-2.png)

After selecting the participants, click on ' **Send SmartFeed**' at the end of the page to successfully send the SmartFeed to the participants.

![After selecting the participants, click on ' Send SmartFeed' at the end of the page to successfully send the SmartFeed to the participants.](/img/helpscout/editors/how-to-send-the-smartfeed-to-teams-3.png)

You can view the **sent history** of a SmartFeed at any time in the future to track which users or groups have received it.

![You can view the sent history of a SmartFeed at any time in the future to track which users or groups have received it.](/img/helpscout/editors/how-to-send-the-smartfeed-to-teams-4.png)
