SmartFeeds enable you to share short, focused snippets of information with your teams. They are designed to help **reinforce critical messages regularly** without requiring lengthy training sessions. SmartFeeds ensure that important information is easily accessible and available for future reference.

## **What Can Be Shared Through SmartFeeds?**

SmartFeeds are ideal for sharing information that your team needs to **remember and apply consistently** in their day-to-day work, such as:

* Updates on the latest changes to products
* Announcements of new product launches
* Information about newly introduced services or offers
* Any critical updates or reminders that teams should retain over time

All SmartFeeds remain available in the feed for ongoing reference.

## Supported File Types for SmartFeeds

You can share a variety of content formats through SmartFeeds, including:

* **Videos** (e.g., product demonstrations, training videos)
* **PDF documents** (e.g., product specification sheets, clinical trial data)
* **PowerPoint presentations** (e.g., product knowledge, features and benefits)
* **Links** to external websites or resources
* **Images or posters**

## How Are SmartFeeds Delivered?

SmartFeeds are displayed as **Twitter-style feeds** within the SmartWinnr mobile application and web interface, making them easy to consume and revisit.

## Notifications for SmartFeeds

When a SmartFeed is published, recipients are automatically notified through:

* **Email notifications**
* **Push notifications** on their mobile devices

This ensures timely awareness and engagement with the shared content.
