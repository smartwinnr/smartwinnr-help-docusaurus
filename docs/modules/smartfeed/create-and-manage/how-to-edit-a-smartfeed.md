Once a SmartFeed has been published, it can be edited if any changes are required.

## Steps to Edit a SmartFeed

* Navigate to the **SmartFeeds** section and open the SmartFeed you want to modify.
* Click the **Edit** option located in the **top-right corner** of the SmartFeed.

[![](/img/helpscout/editors/how-to-edit-a-smartfeed-1.png)](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5fcc5d22d580ce55a38b4a61/images/5ff0a7de495a886ca782954b/file-sOpQVX53IJ.png)

* The **Edit SmartFeed** screen will open, allowing you to update the title and content as needed.

[![](/img/helpscout/editors/how-to-edit-a-smartfeed-2.png)](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5fcc5d22d580ce55a38b4a61/images/5ff0a89f495a886ca782954e/file-q1QVuq9S3y.png)

* After making the required changes, click the **Update** button at the bottom of the page.

Upon clicking **Update**, the SmartFeed will be successfully updated with the latest changes.
