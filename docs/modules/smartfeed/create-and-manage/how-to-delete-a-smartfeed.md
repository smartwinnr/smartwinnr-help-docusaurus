You can delete a SmartFeed even after it has been sent to your teams. Once deleted, the SmartFeed will be **removed from all recipients’ feeds** and **permanently deleted from the system**. Please note that deleting a SmartFeed will also remove all associated **tracking details and sent history**, and this action cannot be undone.

If you want to make a SmartFeed unavailable to recipients **while retaining its view history and tracking data**, we recommend **expiring** the SmartFeed instead of deleting it.

## Steps to Delete a SmartFeed

* Navigate to **EDITOR > LEARNING AND KNOWLEDGE > SmartFeeds**.
* Select the SmartFeed you wish to delete.
* In the **top-right corner**, click the **hamburger (⋮) icon**.
* Choose **Delete** from the menu.

![](/img/helpscout/editors/how-to-delete-a-smartfeed-1.png)

* A confirmation pop-up will appear.

[![](/img/helpscout/editors/how-to-delete-a-smartfeed-2.png)](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5fcc5d22d580ce55a38b4a61/images/5fd11362a72c62318a4c08d9/file-lQvUs6r2N2.png)

* Click **Yes** to confirm the deletion.

Once you delete a SmartFeed, that SmartFeed will disappear from the feed of all recipients.
