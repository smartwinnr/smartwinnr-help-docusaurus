Images or pictures can be sent as content in your SmartFeed or attach to your Question in SmartWinnr.

Click on the image icon to attach an image to your SmartFeed or to the question that you create in SmartWinnr.

![](/img/helpscout/editors/how-to-add-a-image-to-your-content-1.png)

Then click on the 'Choose File' button to select the required files from the computer.

![](/img/helpscout/editors/how-to-add-a-image-to-your-content-2.png)

After uploading the image, click the **Save New SmartFeed** button to save the SmartFeed successfully.
