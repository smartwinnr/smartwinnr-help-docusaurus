End users/sales reps can access the SCORM course from their SmartWinnr account by following the below-given steps:

1\. Go to **"Dashboard>> My Task"**, Here the sales rep will be able to see the SmartPath assigned to them.

![1\. Go to "Dashboard>> My Task", Here the sales rep will be able to see the SmartPath assigned to them.](/img/helpscout/managers-and-team-members/how-to-access-the-scorm-course-from-the-smartwinnr-app-1.png)

2\. Open the SmartPath, it will take you to the following screen. Click on the button with an “**Arrow**” to see the course.

![2\. Open the SmartPath, it will take you to the following screen. Click on the button with an “Arrow” to see the course.](/img/helpscout/managers-and-team-members/how-to-access-the-scorm-course-from-the-smartwinnr-app-2.png)

3\. It will take you to the course. Click on the button “**START PLAYING**” to move to the course.

![3\. It will take you to the course. Click on the button “START PLAYING” to move to the course.](/img/helpscout/managers-and-team-members/how-to-access-the-scorm-course-from-the-smartwinnr-app-3.png)

4\. Click on the button “**START COURSE**”.It will open the course and the sales rep can take it.

![4\. Click on the button “START COURSE”.It will open the course and the sales rep can take it.](/img/helpscout/managers-and-team-members/how-to-access-the-scorm-course-from-the-smartwinnr-app-4.png)
