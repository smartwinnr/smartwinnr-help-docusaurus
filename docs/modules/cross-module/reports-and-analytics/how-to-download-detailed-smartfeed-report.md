To download the SmartFeed detailed report, follow the steps below:

1. **Navigate to SmartFeeds**

    Go to **EDITOR > LEARNING AND KNOWLEDGE>View All SmartFeeds**.

    This page displays the list of all SmartFeeds created in the system.

2. **Access the Download Option**

    Click on the **hamburger menu (three-dot icon)** located at the **top-right corner** of the SmartFeed Page and select the option **D****ownload SmartFeed Detailed report**.

    **Apply Filters (Optional)**

    You can refine the report by applying filters such as:

    * **Date range** during which the SmartFeed was created
    * **Business Unit** under which the SmartFeed was created

        Apply the required filters to narrow down the data.

3. **Download the Report**

    Once the filters are applied, download the report.

    The report will be generated based on the selected filter criteria.

### Downloading SmartFeed Detailed Reports from Other SmartFeeds

In addition to your own SmartFeeds, SmartWinnr also allows you to download detailed reports for **Other SmartFeeds**.

The **Other SmartFeeds** section displays the list of SmartFeeds that were created by **other editors** and to which you have been granted access.

From this section:

* Navigate to **EDITOR > LEARNING AND KNOWLEDGE>View All SmartFeeds**
* Switch to the **Other SmartFeeds** tab
* Click on the **hamburger menu (three-dot icon)** at the top-right corner of the SmartFeed Page
* Select **Download SmartFeed Detailed Report**

You can also apply filters such as **date range** and **business unit** to download the report based on your requirements.

![](/img/helpscout/editors/how-to-download-detailed-smartfeed-report-2.png)

### SmartFeed Detailed Report – Included Information

The **SmartFeed Detailed Report** provides comprehensive information about each SmartFeed and its engagement details. The report includes the following key data points:

* **SmartFeed Name**
* **Category** under which the SmartFeed was created
* **Created By** – basic details of the user who created the SmartFeed
* **Created On** – date of SmartFeed creation

    **Engagement Metrics**, including:

  * Number of **Likes**
  * Number of **Comments**

        **Content Details**, specifying the type of content included in the SmartFeed, such as:

  * PDF documents
  * Images
  * Videos

This report helps administrators and editors track SmartFeed creation, content types, and user engagement effectively.
