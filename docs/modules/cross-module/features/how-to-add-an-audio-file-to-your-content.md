You can send an audio file as part of your SmartFeed or you can attach an audio file to your question created in SmartWinnr.

Click on the audio icon to attach the audio file to your SmartFeed or to the question that you create in SmartWinnr.

![Click on the audio icon to attach the audio file to your SmartFeed or to the question that you create in SmartWinnr.](/img/helpscout/editors/how-to-add-an-audio-file-to-your-content-1.png)

Once you click on the audio icon, the following pop-up comes up.

![Once you click on the audio icon, the following pop-up comes up.](/img/helpscout/editors/how-to-add-an-audio-file-to-your-content-2.png)

Click on the '**Choose File**' button to select the required audio file from your system.

_Kindly note that the audio format should be MP3._
