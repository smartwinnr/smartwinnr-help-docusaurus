> **At a glance** — Enable multiple attempts for quizzes to allow users to retry until they pass.

When **Allow Multiple Attempts** is enabled, users can take a quiz multiple times. If they do not achieve the required pass percentage after exhausting all attempts, the status will show as **Attempted** instead of **Completed**. This helps accurately reflect their progress.

## When to use this
Enable multiple attempts when you want to give users the opportunity to improve their scores on quizzes. This is useful in scenarios like:
- Training programs where mastery is essential.
- Assessments that require a certain level of understanding.
- Situations where users may benefit from retrying to reinforce learning.

## Steps

### 1. Enable Allow Multiple Attempts
Navigate to the quiz settings and toggle the **Allow Multiple Attempts** option. This will permit users to retake the quiz as needed.

### 2. Monitor Attempt Status
Check the status of user attempts in the **Quiz Analytics Page**. Users who do not meet the passing criteria will have their status marked as **Attempted**.

![Quiz analytics](/img/helpscout/authored/how-to-allow-multiple-attempts-mqb8qqb6.png)

### 3. Review Reports
Access the **Quiz User-wise Report** and **Quiz Combined Report** to see how users are performing across multiple attempts.

## Tips
- Encourage users to review their incorrect answers after each attempt to enhance learning.
- Use the analytics data to identify common areas where users struggle and adjust your training materials accordingly.
