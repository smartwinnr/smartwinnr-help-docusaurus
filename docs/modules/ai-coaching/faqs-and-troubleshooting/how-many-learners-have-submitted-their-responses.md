You can find this information in the **Active Learners** metric on the AI Coaching Dashboard.

**Where to look:**

* Navigate to the Coaching Dashboard
* Look at the "Overview Analysis" tab
* Find the **Active Learners** metric in the top section

**What it shows:** The Active Learners number displays the total count of users who have submitted at least one roleplay attempt during your selected time period.

**Example:** If the metric shows "342" with "+10% vs last quarter," this means 342 learners have submitted responses in the current timeframe.

**Important note:** This count only includes learners who have submitted at least one attempt. Users who logged in but didn't submit anything are not counted as Active Learners.

## What else can you track on this dashboard?

In addition to the number of active learners, the same dashboard also shows two other key engagement metrics:

### Avg Attempts

**What it shows:** The average number of roleplay submissions per active learner during your selected time period.

**Example:** If the metric shows "4.8" with "+7% vs last quarter," this means each active learner has submitted an average of 4.8 roleplay attempts in the current timeframe.

**Why this matters:** This tells you how engaged your learners are with practice. Higher numbers indicate learners are committed to improving through repeated practice. Research shows learners typically need 3-5 attempts to reach proficiency in most skills.

### Avg Session

**What it shows:** The average time a learner spends completing one AI roleplay attempt.

**Example:** If the metric shows "8.10 min" with "+4% vs last quarter," this means the typical roleplay session lasts 8 minutes and 10 seconds.

**Why this matters:** This helps you understand whether learners are taking enough time to thoughtfully engage with the roleplay. Most effective sessions range from 7-12 minutes-enough time for meaningful interaction while remaining efficient compared to traditional 30-minute sessions.

* * *

## Adjusting the timeframe

All three metrics update based on your selected time period:

* Click the time filter buttons (30D, 90D, or calendar icon for custom date range) at the top of the dashboard
* All metrics will update to reflect activity within your selected period
* The percentage comparisons show change versus the previous period of equal length
