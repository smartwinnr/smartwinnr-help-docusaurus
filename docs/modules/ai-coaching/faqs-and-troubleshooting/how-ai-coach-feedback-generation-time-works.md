> **At a glance** - AI Coach feedback generation times vary based on evaluation types. Standard evaluations are quicker, while Soft Skills Evaluations take longer.

The **AI Coach** automatically evaluates scenario attempts and generates personalized feedback for learners. Understanding the time it takes for feedback generation helps set expectations for users.

## When to use this
This information is useful when you want to understand the feedback timeline after a scenario attempt. Consider this when:
- You are a learner awaiting feedback on your performance.
- You are a manager checking on your team’s feedback timelines.
- You are an admin managing the AI Coach settings.

## Steps

### 1. Understand standard evaluation times
Standard evaluations typically take **2 to 3 minutes** to process. This is the usual timeframe for generating feedback after a scenario attempt.

![Soft Skills Enabled](/img/helpscout/authored/how-ai-coach-feedback-generation-time-works-mqkhespo.png)

### 2. Learn about Soft Skills Evaluation
When **Soft Skills Evaluation** is enabled, the system performs a detailed frame-by-frame analysis of video recordings and audio interactions. This assessment focuses on communication, presentation, engagement, and other behavioral skills.

### 3. Expect longer feedback times
Due to the advanced processing involved in Soft Skills Evaluation, feedback generation time increases to approximately **10 to 12 minutes**.

## Tips
- Check the evaluation type before expecting feedback to manage your time effectively.
- Communicate with learners about potential feedback delays when Soft Skills Evaluation is active.
- Monitor feedback times to ensure they align with your training objectives.

## Things to know
:::caution
Feedback generation times may vary based on system load and the complexity of the scenario.
:::
