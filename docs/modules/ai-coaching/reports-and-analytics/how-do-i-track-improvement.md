# **SmartWinnr AI Coaching Dashboard**

## What this feature does

The AI Coaching Dashboard gives you a complete view of how your team is performing in AI roleplay practice. It shows participation trends, skill improvement over time, and areas where your learners need additional support.

This dashboard is designed for trainers and editors to monitor coaching activity, track skill development, and identify training opportunities across your organization.

## When should you use this

Use the AI Coaching Dashboard to:

* Monitor how many team members are actively practicing their skills
* Track learning progress and score improvements across multiple attempts
* Identify which skills are improving and which need more focus
* Measure the time savings from using AI coaching instead of traditional roleplay methods
* Review AI-generated insights about learner behavior and performance trends
* Compare performance across different teams, regions, or organizational groups
* Make data-driven decisions about where to focus your coaching efforts

## Navigating the dashboard tabs

The AI Coaching Dashboard has three main tabs at the top:

[**Overview Analysis**](https://help.smartwinnr.com/article/323-understanding-the-overview-tab-on-ai-coaching-dashboard#Overview-Analysis-Tab-sf1XK)

Shows high-level participation metrics and engagement trends

[**Competency & Skills Insights**](https://help.smartwinnr.com/article/324-11#Competency--Skills-Insights-Tab-pjCBA)

Displays detailed skill development and performance by region or group

[**Participation Insights**](./understanding-the-competency-skills-insights-tab-on-the-ai-coaching-dashboard.md#Participation-Insights-Tab-H95ZC)

Provides metrics on session duration and engagement patterns

Click each tab to view different aspects of your coaching program's performance.
