This feature allows editors, trainers, and admins to view video coaching submissions from users in real time. You can see who has submitted videos, review their AI-generated scores and feedback, and track which submissions have been reviewed by coaches or managers.

## When should you use this

Use this feature when you need to:

* Monitor submission activity across your coaching assignments
* Check the progress of individual users
* Review AI evaluations and scores for submitted videos
* See which submissions still need coach review
* Track completion rates for coaching assignments

* * *

## Step-by-step instructions

### Access the Coaching submissions list

1. From the left navigation menu, click **Video Coaching** under the **COACHING** section.
2. Select **View Coaching** from the submenu.
3. You'll see a list of all coaching assignments with the following information:
    * **Coaching Title**: The name of the coaching assignment
    * **Response Submitted**: Shows completion percentage and submission count (e.g., "2/8" means 2 users submitted out of 8 assigned)
    * **Review Done**: Shows review progress (e.g., "1/2" means 1 submission reviewed out of 2 total submissions)
    * **Status**: Whether the coaching is Active, Closed, or Ends in a specific timeframe
    * **Analytics**: Icon to view detailed analytics

### View detailed analytics for a coaching assignment

4. In the **Analytics** column, click the **chart icon** next to the coaching assignment you want to review.
5. The Coaching Analytics page opens, showing:
    * Completion status charts (video submission and coach review progress)
    * Participation breakdown by location or team
    * Average AI scores
    * A detailed table listing all participants

### View an individual user's submission

6. In the participant table at the bottom of the Analytics page, find the user whose submission you want to view.
7. Click on the **Participant Name** (displayed as a blue link).
8. The individual submission page opens, displaying:
    * **Coach name**: The assigned coach or manager who will review this submission
    * **Submission date and time**
    * **Video player**: Watch the submitted video
    * **Attempts dropdown**: If the user submitted multiple times, select which attempt to view

### Review submission details using the tabs

Once you're viewing an individual submission, use the tabs at the top to see different aspects of the evaluation:

* **Scores**: View the overall AI score, response duration, strengths, and improvement areas
* **Features**: Check if the user covered required product features or keywords, and view any banned words detected
* **Analytics**: See speaking pace (words per minute), score trends across attempts, and progress on individual objectives
* **Evaluation Criteria**: Review detailed scoring for each competency or knowledge area the AI evaluated
* **Compliance and Fact Check**: See compliance violations, factual errors, and suggestions for balanced messaging

## What happens next

After viewing submissions:

* You can navigate back to the Coaching Analytics page to view other submissions
* Coaches or managers can use this information to provide additional feedback or coaching
* You can track which submissions still need human review in the "Review Done" column

* * *

## Tips

* The submission count format "2/8" tells you exactly how many users out of your total assigned have completed the coaching
* Use the **Attempts** dropdown to compare a user's improvement across multiple submissions
* The AI provides both strengths and improvement areas automatically, which can guide coaching conversations
* Filter options at the top of the Coaching page (Date Range, Business Unit, Coaching Type, Status) help you narrow down to specific coaching assignments

* * *

## Things to know

* Only submitted videos appear in the Analytics view. Users who haven't submitted yet won't show in the participant table.
* The review count reflects reviews completed by coaches or managers, not the AI evaluation (which happens automatically).
* Scores and feedback are generated by AI immediately upon submission, so you can view them right away without waiting for human review.
