Only Editor who has created the coaching can reset a video coaching assignment.

To reset the coaching video for a user follow below steps:

1. Go to **Editor > Coaching > Video Coaching** > Click on **Analytics** button on the right side.

    ![Go to Editor > Coaching > Video Coaching > Click on Analytics button on the right side.](/img/helpscout/editors/how-to-reset-a-coaching-video-1.jpg)

2. Once you are on the **Analytics** page scroll down to the **Coaching Status** tab and select the submitted video as shown below:

    ![Once you are on the Analytics page scroll down to the Coaching Status tab and select the submitted video as shown below:](/img/helpscout/editors/how-to-reset-a-coaching-video-2.jpg)

3. Once you open the video page click on the hamburger menu on the top right and then click on "**Reset Coaching**" button.

    ![Once you open the video page click on the hamburger menu on the top right and then click on "Reset Coaching" button.](/img/helpscout/editors/how-to-reset-a-coaching-video-3.jpg)

4. Final prompt will appear and click on "**Yes**".

    ![Final prompt will appear and click on "Yes".](/img/helpscout/editors/how-to-reset-a-coaching-video-4.jpg)

This will reset the coaching video for the particular user and redirect you back to the "**Analytics**" page.
