SmartWinnr allows you to **archive SmartPaths** that are no longer required, while still providing the option to **unarchive them in the future** if needed. Archiving helps keep your active list clean and focused, without permanently deleting valuable content.

## **How to Archive a SmartPath**

1. Go to **EDITOR > LEARNING AND KNOWLEDGE > SmartPaths > View All SmartPaths**.

![Go to EDITOR > LEARNING AND KNOWLEDGE > SmartPaths > View All SmartPaths.](/img/helpscout/editors/how-to-archive-a-smartpath-and-unhide-them-in-future-if-needed-1.png)

2. Locate the SmartPath that you want to archive from the list of active SmartPaths.

![Locate the SmartPath that you want to archive from the list of active SmartPaths.](/img/helpscout/editors/how-to-archive-a-smartpath-and-unhide-them-in-future-if-needed-2.png)

3. Now on the top right corner click on hamburger icon and click on "**Archive SmartPath**" option.

![Now on the top right corner click on hamburger icon and click on "Archive SmartPath" option.](/img/helpscout/editors/how-to-archive-a-smartpath-and-unhide-them-in-future-if-needed-3.png)

Once archived, the SmartPath will no longer appear in the active SmartPaths list, but it will remain accessible in the archived section.

## How to Unhide (Restore) an Archived SmartPath

1. Go to **EDITOR > LEARNING AND KNOWLEDGE > SmartPaths > View All SmartPaths**.
2. Click on the hamburger icon on the top right and click on "**Archived SmartPath**"

![Click on the hamburger icon on the top right and click on "Archived SmartPath"](/img/helpscout/editors/how-to-archive-a-smartpath-and-unhide-them-in-future-if-needed-4.png)

3. On that page you can see all the Archived SmartPaths. Open the SmartPath that you need to unarchive.
4. Click on the hamburger icon on the top right and click on "**Unarchived SmartPath**" and that SmartPath will be now available on the SmartPath page.

![Click on the hamburger icon on the top right and click on "Unarchived SmartPath" and that SmartPath will be now available on the SmartPath p](/img/helpscout/editors/how-to-archive-a-smartpath-and-unhide-them-in-future-if-needed-5.png)
