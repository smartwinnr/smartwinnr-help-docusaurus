# Profile & Settings

## Accessing Profile Settings

To access your profile settings:

1. Open the SmartWinnr app and click on the **hamburger icon** at the top left corner
2. Click on your **profile picture** at the top of the menu
3. Click on the **Settings** icon at the top right corner

![Profile Menu](/img/getting-started/helpscout-profile-menu-efc355f6.png)

![Profile Page](https://lh5.googleusercontent.com/AGJCQf4iNzebAOO-YUa1DNepJtYYvRSPP_KYAFINs9SETu3LOsCbNIimsq-6B6ArKBbJlBreKVY4tgQpf1B0ACTI3J-DtrnqCbc3vAORMHm-CKfRKbtELoskYuNLTGucovjy5n-V)

## Changing Your Password

From the Settings page, click on **Change Password**. You will need to enter:

- Your old password
- Your new password
- Confirm your new password

Then click on the **Change Password** button to update your password.

![Change Password Screen](https://lh6.googleusercontent.com/s-P3-RJmoRQ6oScZpClIzqFxpwKMW1RLWZAB-xmfFQPLCl6hAaY9YNeOjAGaidtMGPewwW7MrjlpanbhcXvCgeZN_HZnjSeU6NK0c2xDnuNRQFFBoygyUifnqWyj7JyWj3UdiERM)

**What happens after you change your password?** After successfully changing your password, SmartWinnr will keep you logged in. You don't need to log in again.

## Changing Your Profile Image

### During First Login

You will be asked to add a profile picture the first time you log in to SmartWinnr. Click on the **Camera icon** and choose to either:

- **Open Camera** to take a new picture
- **Open Gallery** to select an existing picture

![First Login Profile Setup](https://lh4.googleusercontent.com/Rc2JPSCbnqg-uUfWsqcuL-LvS2PmHNLm5xr4ZxMNnI7Z8MRF8FYeVqNbiqHPEQTRSyQhjOOcgNys6zHLWCn5n4PbwW0IjWlrENcJI9xh_dXaHvNAyGtU3sWgQ7yBvsF4MFP6_KYk)

### After You're Already Logged In

1. Navigate to your profile page (hamburger menu  profile picture)
2. Click on the **camera icon** on your profile picture
3. Choose either:
   - **Open Camera** to take a new picture
   - **Open Gallery** to select an existing picture

![Profile Picture Change](https://lh4.googleusercontent.com/zvtTITC7qFS29DeFfkJFOhPwQRE1bpKFjydQ45tuAlaI8CJTnHLBd1kLzbDovk_xbn-twTX8dGP9MhXZahSvE6Ia_KXo7f53KAxxQhYcZic_L7Cy60iOnWW7f4vGXw0qRXkk1bsS)

## Changing Your Language

If multiple languages are enabled for your organization, you can change your language from English to any other available language.

From the Settings page:

1. Click on **Change Language**
2. Select the language from the list of available languages for your organization
3. Click **OK** to update your language preference

![Language Settings](/img/getting-started/helpscout-language-settings-e81a26e8.png)

## Managing Push Notifications

Push notifications will alert you on your mobile device every time a new task is assigned to you.

To enable push notifications:

1. From the Settings page, click on **Notification**
2. Tap on the **toggle button** to switch on notifications in SmartWinnr

![Notification Settings](/img/getting-started/helpscout-notification-settings-a53ae8cf.png)

**Note:** This enables push notifications for the mobile app. However, push notifications can still be disabled at the phone level, which will override the app settings. Make sure notifications are also enabled in your phone's settings for the SmartWinnr app.
