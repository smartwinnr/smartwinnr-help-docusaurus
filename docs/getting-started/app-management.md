# App Management

## Checking Your App Version

To check the version of your SmartWinnr app:

1. Open the SmartWinnr app and click on the **hamburger icon** at the top left corner
2. Click on your **profile picture** at the top of the menu  
3. Click on the **gear icon** at the top right corner
4. Go to **App version** to see the version installed on your system

![App Version Check](/img/getting-started/helpscout-app-version-check-c4a072b6.png)

## Updating the SmartWinnr App

### From App Stores (Recommended)

The best way to update your SmartWinnr app is through your device's app store:

**For Android:**
1. Open Google Play Store
2. Search for "SmartWinnr" 
3. If an update is available, you'll see an "Update" button
4. Tap "Update" to install the latest version

**For iOS:**
1. Open the App Store
2. Search for "SmartWinnr"
3. If an update is available, you'll see an "Update" button  
4. Tap "Update" to install the latest version

### From Within the App

You can also check for updates from within the SmartWinnr app:
1. Follow the steps above to check your app version
2. If a newer version is available, the app may prompt you to update
3. Follow the prompts to update to the latest version

### Web View Updates

If you're using SmartWinnr through a web browser, the web view updates automatically. However, you may need to:
- Clear your browser cache
- Refresh the page to get the latest version
- Ensure you're using a supported browser

## System Requirements

### Browser Support

SmartWinnr works best with the latest versions of:
- **Chrome** (Recommended)
- **Firefox** 
- **Edge**

**Note:** Internet Explorer might not support some advanced functionality of SmartWinnr.

### Mobile App OS Support

The SmartWinnr mobile app is supported on:

**Android:**
- Android version 5.0 and above

**iOS:**
- iOS version 11.0 and above

## Troubleshooting App Updates

### App Won't Update
- Ensure you have sufficient storage space on your device
- Check your internet connection
- Try restarting your device and attempting the update again
- If issues persist, contact your system administrator

### App Behaving Strangely After Update
- Try logging out and logging back in
- Clear the app cache (Android) or reinstall the app
- Contact [support@smartwinnr.com](mailto:support@smartwinnr.com) if issues continue

### Password Reset After App Update
After major app updates or reinstallation, you may need to enter your login credentials again. The app will no longer remember your previous password and you'll need to log in fresh.
