# Reports (Legacy)

The original learning and admin report guides — Learner Overall, Quiz-wise
Analytics, Team Analytics, Team Progress, and User Login reports — kept here for
reference while the new module-scoped reporting pages are written.

## What's in this section

- **Learner reports** — Aggregate and per-learner views of activity and mastery.
- **Quiz-wise analytics** — Drill-downs by quiz, category, country, and team.
- **Team analytics and progress** — Manager-flavored views of reportee performance.
- **User login reports** — Active, initial, and not-yet-using-SmartWinnr breakdowns.

Each module also has its own **Reports & Analytics** sub-section under
`Modules → <module> → Reports & Analytics` covering module-specific dashboards.

This section is visible to editors and above whose organization has at least one
of the reporting privileges enabled (`learnerReport`, `adminReports`,
`generatedReports`).
