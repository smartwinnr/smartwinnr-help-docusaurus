# Mobile App Navigation 📱

The SmartWinnr mobile app is designed for intuitive, on-the-go learning. This guide will help you navigate all the key features efficiently.

## App Home Screen

### Dashboard Overview
When you open the app, you'll see your personalized dashboard with:

- **📋 Recent Activities**: Latest quizzes, learning content, and assignments
- **🎯 Progress Summary**: Your completion rates and performance metrics
- **🏆 Achievements**: Recent badges, certificates, and competition rankings
- **📢 Notifications**: Important updates and reminders
- **⚡ Quick Actions**: Fast access to frequently used features

### Navigation Menu
Tap the **hamburger menu** (☰) at the top-left to access:

- **🏠 Home**: Return to dashboard
- **📝 Quizzes**: View and take assigned assessments
- **📚 Learning**: Access SmartFeeds, SmartPaths, and Knowledge Hub
- **🏆 Competitions**: Join challenges and view leaderboards
- **📊 Analytics**: Personal performance and progress tracking
- **⚙️ Settings**: Account preferences and app configuration
- **👤 Profile**: View and edit your profile information

## Key App Sections

### 📝 **Quizzes Section**
Navigate to view and manage your assessments:

**My Quizzes Tab**
- **🟢 Available**: Quizzes ready to take
- **🟡 In Progress**: Partially completed assessments
- **✅ Completed**: Finished quizzes with results
- **⏰ Overdue**: Past-due assignments requiring attention

**Taking a Quiz**
1. Tap on an available quiz
2. Review instructions and time limits
3. Answer questions at your own pace
4. Submit when complete
5. View results and explanations immediately

### 📚 **Learning Section**

**SmartFeeds**
- **📱 Feed View**: Twitter-like stream of bite-sized content
- **🔍 Search**: Find specific topics or information
- **💾 Save for Later**: Bookmark important content
- **❤️ Like & Comment**: Engage with content and colleagues

**SmartPaths**
- **🛤️ Learning Journeys**: Structured, sequential learning experiences
- **📍 Progress Tracking**: See your advancement through each path
- **🎯 Milestones**: Track completion of modules and checkpoints
- **📚 Resources**: Access supporting materials and references

**Knowledge Hub (KHub)**
- **🗂️ Browse Categories**: Explore organized content folders
- **🔍 Intelligent Search**: Find documents, videos, and resources quickly
- **⬇️ Download**: Save content for offline access
- **🆕 Recent & Trending**: Discover popular and newly added content

### 🏆 **Competitions Section**

**Active Competitions**
- **🎮 Join Challenges**: Participate in individual or team competitions
- **📊 Leaderboards**: View your ranking and team performance
- **🎯 Activities**: Complete tasks to earn points and badges
- **⏱️ Time Remaining**: Track competition deadlines

**My Achievements**
- **🏅 Badges Earned**: View your collection of achievements
- **📜 Certificates**: Access earned certificates and credentials
- **📈 Points History**: Track your scoring over time
- **🏆 Rank Progression**: See your competitive improvement

### 📊 **Analytics Section**

**Personal Dashboard**
- **📈 Performance Trends**: Track improvement over time
- **🎯 Category Strengths**: See where you excel
- **🔍 Areas for Improvement**: Identify knowledge gaps
- **⏱️ Learning Time**: Monitor time spent on activities

**Detailed Reports**
- **📝 Quiz Performance**: Question-by-question analysis
- **📚 Learning Progress**: SmartPath and content completion
- **🏆 Competition History**: Past challenge performance
- **📊 Comparative Analytics**: How you compare to peers

## Mobile-Specific Features

### 📱 **Offline Access**
**Download Content**
1. Navigate to the content you want to save
2. Tap the **download icon** (⬇️)
3. Content saves to your device for offline viewing
4. Access downloaded content even without internet

**Sync When Online**
- Progress automatically syncs when you reconnect
- Completed offline activities update your records
- New content downloads when available

### 🔔 **Push Notifications**
**Notification Types**
- **📝 New Assignments**: When quizzes or content are assigned
- **⏰ Deadlines**: Reminders before due dates
- **🎉 Achievements**: When you earn badges or complete milestones
- **🏆 Competition Updates**: Leaderboard changes and new challenges
- **📢 Announcements**: Important updates from your organization

**Managing Notifications**
1. Go to **Settings** → **Notifications**
2. Toggle notification types on/off
3. Set quiet hours if desired
4. Choose notification sounds and vibration preferences

### 🔍 **Search Functionality**
**Global Search**
- Use the **search icon** in the top navigation
- Search across all content types: quizzes, learning materials, KHub documents
- Filter results by content type, date, or category
- Save frequent searches for quick access

**Smart Suggestions**
- App learns from your usage patterns
- Suggests relevant content based on your learning history
- Recommends quizzes and SmartPaths for skill development
- Highlights trending content in your areas of interest

## Navigation Tips & Shortcuts

### ⚡ **Quick Actions**
- **Swipe Left/Right**: Navigate between sections
- **Pull to Refresh**: Update content in any section
- **Long Press**: Access additional options on content items
- **Double Tap**: Quick like/save on SmartFeeds
- **Pinch to Zoom**: Zoom in on images, documents, or charts

### 🎨 **Personalization Options**
**Theme & Display**
- Choose between light and dark themes
- Adjust font sizes for better readability
- Customize dashboard layout and widget preferences
- Set language preferences for the interface

**Quick Access**
- **Favorites**: Star frequently used content for easy access
- **Recently Viewed**: Quick return to recent activities
- **Bookmarks**: Save important pages and resources
- **Custom Shortcuts**: Create shortcuts to frequently used features

### 🔄 **Sync & Updates**
**Keep App Updated**
- Enable automatic app updates in your device settings
- Check for updates regularly for new features
- Clear app cache if experiencing issues
- Log out and back in to refresh your session

**Data Management**
- Monitor data usage in mobile settings
- Use Wi-Fi for large downloads when possible
- Clear downloaded content periodically to free space
- Backup important progress before major app updates

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

### 🐛 **App Performance**
- **Slow Loading**: Check internet connection, clear app cache
- **Crashes**: Restart app, update to latest version
- **Sync Issues**: Log out and back in, check network connectivity
- **Missing Content**: Refresh the section, contact support if persistent

### 🔐 **Access Problems**
- **Can't Log In**: Check credentials, try password reset
- **Missing Features**: Verify your role permissions with administrator
- **Content Not Available**: Check if content is assigned to you
- **Notification Issues**: Verify notification settings both in app and device settings

For additional help with mobile app navigation, contact your system administrator or [support@smartwinnr.com](mailto:support@smartwinnr.com).
