Follow the below steps to upload your video recording for the coaching assignment. 

Step:1 Go to the Left menu > Coachings > Assigned tab. Select the coaching assignment.

![](/img/coaching/helpscout-image-0aff2a3a.png)

Step:2 On the coaching details page, click on the **“Record Video”** button to record your video.

![](/img/coaching/helpscout-image-2dd5f254.png)Step:3 Here you can click on the camera icon and record the video which you would like to submit for the coaching.

![](/img/coaching/helpscout-image-87398200.png)Step:4 Once the video is recorded successfully. Kindly click on the **“Upload Recorded File”** button to upload that video file.

![](/img/coaching/helpscout-image-c8711cd0.png)Step:5 After that you will get a confirmation screen like the one below, once your video recording gets uploaded successfully.

![](/img/coaching/helpscout-image-69fa7475.png)
