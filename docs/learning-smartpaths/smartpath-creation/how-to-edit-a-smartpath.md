Follow the below steps to edit a SmartPath that is already created:

Go to **EDITOR >ÂLEARNING AND KNOWLEDGE >ÂSmartPaths > View All SmartPaths.** You will get to the following page where a list of all the SmartPaths that have been created are displayed:

[![](/img/learning/helpscout-image-ebf41726.png)](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5fcc5d22d580ce55a38b4a61/images/6016a10d2042ff6d1b2a9043/file-kfMOibsmCo.png)

On the particular SmartPath card, _click_ on the **'****Edit'**Âbutton at the bottom right corner. Alternatively, you can click and open a particular SmartPath and then click on the **'****Edit SmartPath****'** button at the top right corner of the screen.

[![](/img/learning/helpscout-image-aa029942.png)](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5fcc5d22d580ce55a38b4a61/images/6016a2ad6867724dfc6ede22/file-1GkzgLJ70X.png)

It will open the following page where you can make changes to the SmartPath:

[![](/img/learning/helpscout-image-48110fa4.png)](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5fcc5d22d580ce55a38b4a61/images/6016a35e1f25b9041beb9bf4/file-mBQ4KA7iVQ.png)

Once you make all the desired changes, _click_ on the '**Update**' button and the SmartPath's changes will get saved successfully.
