## Multiple Language Support

Are your documents in different languages? Don't worry. SmartWinnr supports documents in multiple languages. So, upload documents in different languages for teams from different countries.

## Intelligent Search

SmartWinnr is powered by a highly intelligent, intuitive, and accurate search. It helps your teams to search through a huge number of documents quickly and easily.

## Offline Access

SmartWinnr provides offline access to these documents. But only for documents that you select. It wont eat up more space on your phone than whats needed.
