SmartWinnr allows you to make a form submission via chat if the form is added to the chat group in which you are part. To make a submission for the form from chat, you can follow the below steps:

Go to Dashboard >> Chat.

![](https://lh6.googleusercontent.com/1i7_oiXyHWS9lDS_2o5KWy3jNrdN94Ef6MTOd8yyiCpSaiFl1j0yzwEIk8wtadJRDzKGsD4gGNBN2i5lwN5QxxDO8LaEQPRNpZ07_NBBnzLQ3HzAjQ-F5UJ6r4lAJci1YXZyhDjb)

An alternate way to select chat is  Go to Left Menu>>Chat.

![](https://lh5.googleusercontent.com/CI1xldqgrP9qcMJEhCAcOTOYCgrafQIC3N5HG_gM9pb6ySxeLih2NfTxVogPGr_l6Hr_bD1btT4asfGuz_ldTtE3D81oGPHoIry2i3ZshDHpEMIG-gpLl_QbaGmgMANrbzKZ2ea3)

Here you can find the chat groups. Clicking on the group will take you to all the conversation and submissions made from the selected chat group.

![](https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/-hFRcjMVV9J6iiwzppOO08lX226RMEsXpZ3hO9J2vfBtpVLhAPLMsRX1KxGytntPstpwqT6ZkN44I6lHylE6V0ONuOGBen_pYgUZ7tQYKprzW_ykCaTo_BDAciUynCHFdfg9Uct1)

Here you can also communicate with other group members and make a submission to the forms assigned to you in SmartWinnr.

> Kindly note that you will find the form in chat only if the form is added to the chat group by your trainer/editor.

Click on the 'Form' icon at the bottom right corner to add a new submission.

![](https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/ETdHZqakm14Ro0A3k1yxKsAwkI96eDPYmtqOVP8VzlNis5i4YGqz-ylp99_mvcW0_15X_tGqcbB_OKHlbiOU3YqaEmPhxO6uN5rfaP-JJ9CKbvQueMR5IDr60jmzEFi3PRgt8_Dk)

Enter the necessary details and click on the  Save button to submit the details.

![](https://lh5.googleusercontent.com/OBjFeKEHBKhAd8r86rBMrd9qgsbp1rHPM7QXm8wMPQP-MyK2anwXh5URbIrnvROZcpff1bwIz26wZpKh4G3aDQO9a6mz4QlJmki6ZwakFrn0dx2s_uonQox54zVY4wmVzZs2SFsx)

After successful submission, you can find the submission details in the chat.

![](https://lh5.googleusercontent.com/DCF41-iEYC8Faq4JlM8HRM3DbOU_wu5_AW8xITMiKfRYoYF0G8erDfXA6X7D7jKcKPV_ZhgUha9bAnDI4rfMM4qxGaGNbEz3moB7k-QopngovHJ0Ns8rP9B1_IML8RuN71UZl9yX)
