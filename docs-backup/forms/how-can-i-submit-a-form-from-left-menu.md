You can Follow the below steps to submit a form in SmartWinnr from Left Menu.

Go to Left Menu>>Forms.

![](https://lh6.googleusercontent.com/K952_jjjZrdHgyHBdp22RjizAM2g-Mo-_Zew9Iuzb4vvjSw0SRdpwgCDSYFCH1bwGG6X9cHyP-FGLvX_rGce6oWG-_s5QztkCoB6ZrA10uTxrFWN7I80km26s1A0kV1hHGk-R5Dn)

Here you can find the forms assigned to you in SmartWinnr. Click on the desired form that you would like to submit now.

![](https://lh6.googleusercontent.com/GJ2Cuv9IZecA0vwWieniWLKvLQocEtiLqL5epl2KsbBAYsCbdBZpPwQ4JvXUmiZt_5y7D1cnpGZyUFJeLrD3QRF0A2ywTzP8FV44K2DAjwMAtM0PZPk5qn-m2AUKP3KMGXDhwxlZ)

This will take you to the submissions that you have made for the selected form.

Click on the plus icon at the bottom right corner to add a new submission.

![](https://lh6.googleusercontent.com/kdVocyVX2lpjp8a-bGoGCJ2ESGlBM6j3QsFa_Uo0E_tsAcVxpJNtZwDMGsF0XvC9smqH_I_EN8rS2w14KZ4XVdT_I4QzmcrPMbM1-d-CNLF3V6XVx8kdsNFwp5wrT5_aA_Ky3qPt)

 Fill in the details for all the required fields and click on the Save button to submit the details. ![](https://lh5.googleusercontent.com/xDqyYtMgGbYUhtyaQ9xeUvHLJ87dMVA0OCXYgSIaXToRqg-OI3zCLwG7evdIe1fPaA-4hEi6pQFvufz2m0VFnXrCwvRYr3yBFP4u-60uIv-YnkITjhJooIvWwNcZtRk6AiLn-3Gu) You will get a pop-up message after the successful submission.![](https://lh6.googleusercontent.com/5002rL3BhUZRYhGY58U2qMU4PRBXHV9B871R0V1V8OofNYcf6YMNFEWmN3xoVlPHNeXFZg7WS0GmqHL67UWjRKhakwC-G5P_QIuXRf-dx9Mz7a0wlXkhE2LiOt5WNrPkbb_ub5Bq)
