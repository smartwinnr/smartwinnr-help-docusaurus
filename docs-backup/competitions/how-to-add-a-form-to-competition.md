Here are the steps to add a Form to a competition:

1.  Once you get to the 4th step of creating competition, select Form in the "Select Task".
2.  Give name to the Form.
3.  Set a start and end date of the task

Below is a screenshot of adding a Form to a competition:

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5fcc5d22d580ce55a38b4a61/images/600a93eb1c64ad47e4b71e06/file-eI1W9nu6pD.png)Select the desired Form you wish to add to the competition.Then, click on the "Create" button.The Form will be added to the competition successfully.
