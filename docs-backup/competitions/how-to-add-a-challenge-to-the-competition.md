Here are the steps to add a challenge to a competition:

1.  Once you get to the 4th step of creating competition, select Challenge in the "Select Task".
2.  Give a name to the task 
3.  Select the challenge from the list of challenges created.

Below is a screenshot of adding a challenge to a competition:

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5fcc5d22d580ce55a38b4a61/images/5ff554dfb5efec03af3f0524/file-7R3aMXvaKD.png)

Then, click on the "Create" button.  The challenge will be added to the competition successfully.  

Once the challenge is created, you can see the challenge score on the leaderboard as well.

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5fcc5d22d580ce55a38b4a61/images/60a8b7901df47a61c5ad4371/file-7FptCdhvGY.jpg)

The score can be viewed for the individual users and also for the team as well.
