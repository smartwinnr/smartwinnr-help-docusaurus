Follow the below steps to upload your video recording for the coaching assignment. 

Step:1 Go to the Left menu > Coachings > Assigned tab. Select the coaching assignment.

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5fcc5d22d580ce55a38b4a61/images/6423b0e85b17ef2714cec74b/file-jGqIdCyPD2.png)

Step:2 On the coaching details page, click on the **“Record Video”** button to record your video.

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5fcc5d22d580ce55a38b4a61/images/64236ee469becd1202b9f36f/file-kMm0DlijEf.png)Step:3 Here you can click on the camera icon and record the video which you would like to submit for the coaching.

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5fcc5d22d580ce55a38b4a61/images/64236f415d17363e673be089/file-SnICbEKjbu.png)Step:4 Once the video is recorded successfully. Kindly click on the **“Upload Recorded File”** button to upload that video file.

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5fcc5d22d580ce55a38b4a61/images/64236fdd5d17363e673be08b/file-ZkHIVmZg76.png)Step:5 After that you will get a confirmation screen like the one below, once your video recording gets uploaded successfully.

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5fcc5d22d580ce55a38b4a61/images/64236ff6e11bbe23cb6ac91e/file-SfwHLQOvAs.png)
