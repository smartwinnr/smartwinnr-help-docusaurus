## Below are the steps to check the version of your SmartWinnr app

Open the SmartWinnr app and click on the **hamburger** icon at the top left corner of the screen. It will show a left menu bar as shown in the screenshot below:

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5fcc5d22d580ce55a38b4a61/images/66d0c7a26b27c01e0fc68d68/file-MtHUUrarNH.png)

Here, click on the **profile picture** at the top. It will take you to the following screen. Click on Settings icon at the top right corner which is highlighted in red. 

Go to the option **App version**. It will take you to the following screen where you can find the version of the SmartWinnr app installed in your system.

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5fcc5d22d580ce55a38b4a61/images/66d0c8236eb51e63b8f9fbba/file-gwh8xNBIcl.png)

### Below are the steps to check the version of your SmartWinnr Web View.

Login the SmartWinnr web view and click on the **profile picture** at the top. It will take you to the following screen. Click on Settings icon at the top right corner which is highlighted in red.

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5fcc5d22d580ce55a38b4a61/images/66d0c9ec954af05808fe8f1a/file-7sQ4uvNDT4.png)

  

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5fcc5d22d580ce55a38b4a61/images/66d0ca426eb51e63b8f9fbc1/file-rsWajKUMj6.png)

Go to option **App version**. It will take you to the above screen where you can find the version of the SmartWinnr web view in your system.
