End users/sales reps can access the SCORM course from their SmartWinnr account by following the below-given steps:

1\. Go to **"Dashboard>> My Task"**, Here the sales rep will be able to see the SmartPath assigned to them.

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5fcc5d22d580ce55a38b4a61/images/62b41e7bdd8e986297c68d40/file-g0hUin9F3v.png)

2\. Open the SmartPath, it will take you to the following screen. Click on the button with an “**Arrow**” to see the course.

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5fcc5d22d580ce55a38b4a61/images/62b479e0360964399e46a388/file-xlJE2BUuD1.png)

3\. It will take you to the course. Click on the button “**START PLAYING**” to move to the course.

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5fcc5d22d580ce55a38b4a61/images/62b41f6b0729834c424f9054/file-dDIyy5a2yo.png)

4\. Click on the button “**START COURSE**”.It will open the course and the sales rep can take it.

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5fcc5d22d580ce55a38b4a61/images/62b4207fa43c1525b680d1e9/file-DialDqqIGW.png)
