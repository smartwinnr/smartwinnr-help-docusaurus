Follow the below steps to enable a chat group:

Go to EDITOR > Settings > Groups. Click on a group. Then click on the hamburger icon at the top right corner of the screen then click on the "Enable Chat" button.

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5fcc5d22d580ce55a38b4a61/images/60c0abc9a6d12c2cd643e0f4/file-mWEdGpFy3N.jpg)

Also, we have an option to enable/disable notification to the chat. If you disable it, No one from the group will get any notification when a message comes in that chat group.

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5fcc5d22d580ce55a38b4a61/images/60c0aaf66264f06fc02ff4f9/file-ygDfqGIu0d.jpg)

Click on the Edit Overview icon and there you can check the Enable Chat Notifications.
