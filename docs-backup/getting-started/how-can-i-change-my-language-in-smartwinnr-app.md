If multiple languages are enabled for your organization in SmartWinnr, you can change your language from English to any other languages available in SmartWinnr. Following are the steps to change the language for your SmartWinnr app.

Open the SmartWinnr app and click on the **hamburger icon** at the top left corner of the screen. It will show a left menu bar as shown in the screenshot below:

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5fcc5d22d580ce55a38b4a61/images/5fce32e3eb7cc612aa355745/file-4RBDtAV2fA.png)

Here, click on the **profile picture** at the top. It will take you to the following screen: 

![](https://lh5.googleusercontent.com/AGJCQf4iNzebAOO-YUa1DNepJtYYvRSPP_KYAFINs9SETu3LOsCbNIimsq-6B6ArKBbJlBreKVY4tgQpf1B0ACTI3J-DtrnqCbc3vAORMHm-CKfRKbtELoskYuNLTGucovjy5n-V)

Click on the _Settings_ icon at the top right corner highlighted in red then go to the option **Change Language**. It will take you to the following screen where you can select the language from the list of languages available for your organization. Then click on the **OK** option to update the language for your SmartWinnr account.

![](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/5fcc5d22d580ce55a38b4a61/images/5fce3493d580ce55a38b4e34/file-ayQt403TI7.png)
