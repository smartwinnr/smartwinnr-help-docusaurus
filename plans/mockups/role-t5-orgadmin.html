Org-wide tooling for OrgAdmins

OrgAdmin unlocks organization-level configuration: which modules are enabled, privilege matrix, custom notifications, CRM components, the transformation layer, incentive planning, and HR-flavored modules. The lamadmin role maps onto this same tier.

What this role sees Everything an admin sees, plus a new Org-wide tools group inside Administration covering 10+ privilege-gated areas. Most entries here are privilege-gated — the org has to enable them for the link to appear, even at this tier.
