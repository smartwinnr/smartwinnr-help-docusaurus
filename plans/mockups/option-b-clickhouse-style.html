A topic-first information architecture inspired by ClickHouse. Whatever your role, you find what you need by what you're trying to do, not by who you are.

Option B — Pure Topic Taxonomy. Top nav is strictly topic-based: Get Started · Guides · Modules · Integrations · Administration · Reports · Reference · Release Notes. Roles & privileges silently hide items the viewer can't access, but never drive the labels.
