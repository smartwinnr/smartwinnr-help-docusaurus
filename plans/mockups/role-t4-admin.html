Create & manage users

Admins gain access to the full Administration tree (users, groups, divisions, audit, banners, approval flow) and the Integrations tab for SSO, SCIM, xAPI, and SCORM configuration.

What this role sees Two extra top-nav tabs unlock here: Integrations and Administration. Everything T3 (Editor) sees is still available — admins are the first tier that can configure the system itself, not just author content for it.
