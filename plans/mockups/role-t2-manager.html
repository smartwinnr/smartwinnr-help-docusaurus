Welcome, manager

You're seeing the SmartWinnr help center filtered to the Manager role. You see everything a learner does, plus dedicated team-management content: coaching reviews, KPI dashboards, reportee analytics, and 1:1 workflows.

What this role sees Manager is a first-class tier — not just "learner plus a few extras." A separate Manager workflows group lives in Guides, every module exposes a "team view" page, and a top-nav tab for Reports & Analytics (scoped to direct + indirect reportees) appears.
