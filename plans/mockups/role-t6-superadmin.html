Superadmin — unrestricted view

Superadmin sees everything every other tier sees, plus a dedicated Superadmin section in the top nav for platform operations, chat-log inspection, vector ops, debug, and the privilege-gate inspector. Privilege gating is bypassed.

What this role sees PRIVILEGE_BYPASS_ROLES = ['superadmin'] in src/access-policy.ts:59. That means even when PRIVILEGE_GATING_ENABLED = true, modules whose privilege the org has not enabled still render for superadmin. The matching server-side guard in server.js uses the shared isAllowedServer() from shared/access-policy.cjs, so URL access matches sidebar visibility 1:1.
